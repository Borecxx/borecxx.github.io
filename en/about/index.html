    • The smart way of living

    Zug Property Development gmbh was founded in 2017 as a reaction to the market demand for affordable quality housing in Switzerland. We have a network of expert Architects and Builders who have many years of experience and have worked on numerous projects with many happy owners.

    Company Concept

    Too often developers are attempting to maximize their profits by building large apartment complexes. Houses are considered luxury objects and are typically price restrictive. As there is a huge demand by consumers to have their own four walls and garden, our concept is to build affordable, quality homes in beautiful locations to fill this gap in the market.
